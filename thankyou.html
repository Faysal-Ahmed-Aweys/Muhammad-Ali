About the Author

My name is Faysal Aweys. I have always been interested in history, especially history about particular people. I am always passionate about hearing stories, reading and also watching documentries and movies about hoistory of people. Learning from them and getting inspired by them to try and better myself in anyway possible is one of the main reasons why I am passionate about history.
