About the Developer

My name is Faysal Aweys. I have always been interested in history, especially history about particular people. I am always passionate about hearing stories, reading and also watching documentries and movies about history of people. Learning from them and getting inspired by them to try and better myself in anyway possible is one of the main reasons why I am passionate about history.

Muhammad Ali is one of the people whose history has been talked to me about by many people. He has inspired many with his courage, Resilience, Humbleness, selflessness and many other qualities. I for one have always heard people talk about him and I ever since had the urge to find out more about him. I started to learn more about his history and decided to make a website about him.

I am still interested to know more about him. Also, to know about other people of history who are inspiring and would be helpful to read about.
